How to Use the Property Tax Calculator

🔍

Search by Address

Type any New York State address in the search box. Suggestions will appear as you type. Select your address to see the property tax rates for that location.

🏫

School District Filter

Many towns have multiple school districts. If your address falls in a town with multiple districts, use the school district dropdown to select the correct one for your property.

💰

Estimate Your Tax Bill

Enter your assessed property value to get an estimated annual tax bill. The bill is broken down by school district, county, municipal, and village components.

⚖️

Compare Locations

Use the Compare section below to add multiple locations and compare their tax rates side by side with a bar chart.

📊

Rank Municipalities

Click the "Rank Municipalities" button at the top to rank counties, towns, cities, villages, or school districts by tax rate within selected counties.

📖

Understanding Rates

Rates are shown as both % (percentage of assessed value) and $ per $1,000 of assessed value. For example, a rate of 2.87% means $28.70 per $1,000 assessed value.
